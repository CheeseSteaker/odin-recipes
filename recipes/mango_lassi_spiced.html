Spiced Mango Lassi

Description

This delicous drink has its roots from India, a land of mouth watering food, and that's not just from all the spices! A drink such as this goes great with breakfast, lunch, diner, or even a snack. Add it to your next meal or just when you need a treat to carry you to that next meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup non-fat plain yogurt
  • 1 cup frozon mango chunks
  • 1/4 cup mango juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender and process untill liquefied
